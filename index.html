Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to help you with any questions you might have about our products, services, or policies. Find quick answers to our most commonly asked questions below.

What Makes Us Stand Out from the Rest?

+

We offer top-quality products, unique styles, and unbeatable service, ensuring a smooth and satisfying shopping experience. Our curated collections are sourced from ethical manufacturers, and we pride ourselves on our responsive customer support team.

What is Your Return & Exchange Policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in original condition with tags attached. Exchanges can be processed online or through our customer service team. Please note that sale items and intimates are final sale for hygiene reasons.

How Long Does Shipping Take?

+

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping times vary by location, generally taking 7-14 business days. All orders include tracking information.

Do You Offer Size Guides for Your Products?

+

Yes, detailed size guides are available on each product page. We provide measurements in inches and centimeters to help you find your perfect fit. If you're between sizes or need additional guidance, our customer service team is happy to assist with specific recommendations.

Are There Any Discounts or Loyalty Programs?

+

We offer a 10% discount on your first order when you subscribe to our newsletter. Our loyalty program rewards you with points for every purchase, which can be redeemed for exclusive discounts. We also run seasonal sales and special promotions throughout the year.

How Can I Track My Order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account on our website to view real-time updates on your order status. If you have any concerns about your shipment, our customer service team is available to help.
